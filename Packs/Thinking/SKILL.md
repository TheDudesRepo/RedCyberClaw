# Thinking Pack

Structured analytical frameworks for security work. Use these before, during, and after engagements to challenge assumptions and find blind spots.

## Capabilities

- Red team critique of attack plans, defenses, and architectures
- First principles decomposition of complex security problems
- Adversarial thinking from multiple attacker perspectives
- Assumption challenging and blind spot identification
